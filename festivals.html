FESTIVALS OF INDIA

Onam

Onam is an annual harvest and Hindu cultural festival celebrated mostly by the people of Kerala.A major annual event for Keralites,it is the official festival of the state.

Holi

Holi is a popular and significant Hindu festival celebrated as the festival of colours,love,and spring.

Diwali

Diwali is the Hindu festival of lights,with variations celebrated in other Indian religions.
